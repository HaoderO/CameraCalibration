Settings / Shortcuts

You may change the default values for some of the move / resize shortcuts.

To do that -- go to Preferences / Shortcuts.

By default the Arrow keys are moving / resizing by 1 pixel, the "fast move" (Command/Alt + Arrow) moves with 100 pixels and the "resize more" (Command/Alt + Shift + Arrow) will resize with 10 pixels.

Change it to whatever you want or load defaults by clicking the Defaults button.

On the same page you can also enable the top-right "menu" button.

This will show small "menu" link at the top-right corner of the ruler. When clicked, this "menu" will open the program's context menu.
